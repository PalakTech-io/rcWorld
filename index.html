The Concept

REAL MACHINES.
REMOTE CONTROL.

RC WORLD is a first-of-its-kind remote operation entertainment platform where users worldwide can take the controls of real, full-scale RC hydraulic machinery — excavators, dump trucks, loaders, and breakers — from anywhere on the planet.

Book a session. Choose your machine. Select your camera angle. Start digging. Watch yourself live on YouTube or Twitch. Our professionally designed job site arena becomes your personal sandbox — and the world's entertainment.

With Raspberry Pi 5 onboard computers, real hydraulic mechanics, and multi-angle HD cameras, every session is broadcast-quality and completely unique.
